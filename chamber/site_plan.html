Japan Cultural Turism

Site Name

Japan Cultural Turism – This name was chosen to reflect the site’s mission of promoting cultural, business, and tourism opportunities in Japan. The word “Chamber” communicates that it is a professional and community-focused organization.

Optional domain: japanchamber.org

Site Purpose

The site will serve as a central hub for residents, tourists, and businesses interested in Japanese culture and commerce. It will include information on events, local attractions, member businesses, and resources for visitors.

Scenarios

  • What cultural events are happening in Japan this month?
  • Where can I find details about joining the Chamber?

Color Schema

Main colors:

  • Green (#004d40) – used for headings, navigation, and accents.
  • White (#ffffff) – used for page backgrounds and sections.

Typography

  • Merriweather – for headings and titles.
  • Open Sans – for body text.
